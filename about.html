Our Story

Born from Fire & Passion

Fifteen years of craft, community, and unforgettable meals.

Ember & Salt was founded in 2009 with a single belief: great dining is equal parts food and feeling. Our founder, Marco Villani, spent a decade in Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe before returning home to open a restaurant that felt personal — not performative.

Today, we source from over 20 local farms, partner with independent fishermen, and write our menu around what's exceptional that week — not what's convenient. Every plate that leaves our kitchen is one we're proud to serve.
